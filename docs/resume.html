about me

my name is roland.

i am studying computer science at Penn State Behrend in Erie, Pennsylvania, with the intent to go into website design & development.

in my studies, i have become comfortable in working with html, css and javascript as well as different web technologies such as nodeJS and express as well as services such as digitalocean.

outside of the classroom, i love to spend my time making things!

online presence

online, i use the alias riley as a name for all of my creative works and community involvement.

i am deeply involved in the online space for people that play rhythm games, which are video games focused on playing along to the beat of a song.

in the rhythm game scene, i am a regional administrator of an online chat server of over 500 people in the Pennsylvania area. as a regional administrator, i help organize tournaments, facilitate community interactions and help with further growth of our scene.

creative works

music production


i have been learning music production since about 2015, primarily using software such as FL Studio, Famitracker and Renoise. i have slowly narrowed my avenue of production to genres such as chiptune, hardcore electronic and hi-tech. these genres are closely related to genres commonly heard in rhythm games, and as a result, over the years my music has been picked up by several commercial rhythm game projects.

game development

along with the rhythm games listed above, i also lead production on a rhythm game of my own, titled EX-XDRiVER.

i have been designing this game since 2019, and have since pulled together a talented team of programmers, music producers, illustrators and designers to help me bring this idea to life.

development started december of 2022 and we are working steadily towards a hopeful release in late 2024 or early 2025.

check out our website for links to our socials, as well as a link to join our our community Discord server with over 350 members!
