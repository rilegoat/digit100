EX-XDRiVER is a game i have been conceptualizing since 2019 and developing since the end of 2022.

it is a rhythm game, which is a video game that requires you to sync your inputs to the beat of the music that is playing along---but what makes us unique is our incorporation of racing game elements, such as steering and going for competitive completion times.

our game is entering into steam early access in early 2024, and will be fully released entirely for free by early 2025.
